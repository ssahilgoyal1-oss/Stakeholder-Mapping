## 📌 What is Stakeholder Mapping?
**Stakeholder Mapping** is a Design Thinking tool used to identify, analyse,
and prioritise all individuals or groups who are affected by or have
influence over a project. It helps teams focus their communication and
engagement strategies on the right people at the right time.

## 🗺️ Stakeholder Power-Interest Grid

| Quadrant | Strategy | Key Stakeholders |
|---|---|---|
| 🔴 High Power + High Interest | **Manage Closely** | Bank CEO, RBI Regulators, IT Team, Cybersecurity |

| 🔵 Low Power + Low Interest | **Monitor** | General Public, Media, Third-party Vendors |

---

## 💡 Key Design Thinking Insights
- **Critical Stakeholder:** RBI & Government Regulators must approve all
  digital banking changes — their buy-in is essential
- **Pain Point:** Senior Citizens face digital literacy barriers and need
  special onboarding support
- **Opportunity:** Small Business Owners benefit most from 24/7 digital
  banking — key advocates for the project
- **Risk:** Cybersecurity Department must be involved from Day 1 to
  prevent data breaches during digitalization
